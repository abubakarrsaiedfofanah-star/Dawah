Beginner Prayer Guide

This sample resource is stored locally in the UMMA University Dawah Team system, so it opens even without internet.

Before Prayer

Prepare with cleanliness, intention, and focus. Make wudu before salah and choose a clean place to pray.

Daily Prayers

The five daily prayers are Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha. Check the UMMA University Dawah Team prayer timetable for current times.

Need Help?

Contact the religious affairs team or welfare team for guidance and support.
