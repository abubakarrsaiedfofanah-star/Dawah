About Us

Who We Are

UMMA University Dawah Team is a community organization dedicated to serving Muslim students and staff at UMMA University. We are committed to fostering spiritual growth, academic excellence, and community support.

Our association brings together students from various disciplines, creating a united platform for Islamic practice and mutual support.

  • Supporting faith-based student life
  • Organizing religious events and activities
  • Providing welfare and counseling services
  • Bridging academic excellence with Islamic values

What We Do

Spiritual Support

Regular prayer gatherings, Jumu'ah services, and Islamic lectures to strengthen faith and spiritual growth among members.

Events & Activities

Organize seminars, workshops, social gatherings, and educational events that promote Islamic knowledge and community bonding.

Welfare Support

Provide financial assistance, counseling, and support services to members facing hardship or personal challenges.

Education

Offer resources for Islamic learning, including Quran study circles, Islamic history seminars, and knowledge-sharing sessions.

Community Service

Engage in volunteering and community outreach programs to benefit society and reflect Islamic values of compassion.

Leadership

Develop leadership skills and prepare members for roles in guiding and inspiring the Muslim student community.
